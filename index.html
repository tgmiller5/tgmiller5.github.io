About Jessica Morrison

“Let's make friends with the cool kids just this once, she says, and doesn't blink at all.” — Hallie and Henry, Say Hi

Jessica Morrison is what happens when grace and hustle meet. She's one part side-eye, and three parts just excited to be here.

Jessica spends her days reporting and writing about science and technology policy for Chemical & Engineering News, the weekly news magazine of the American Chemical Society. She's excited about creating memorable user experiences, building community on-line and off, and changing the culture of work.

There's a good chance you've seen her at a #DCTech event. Say hi. ❤︎
