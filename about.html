About Tammy


Currently, Tammy Barbee is a Senior Information Technology Support Specialist where she works with end users on software and hardware needs including installing specific software remotely.
Tammy also has two other roles at her current job in which she maintains daily trends on the tickets that are assigned to Tier 2 and she provides monthly training.

Tammy Barbee has a Master degree in Management Information Systems from Strayer University and a Bachelor of Business Administration in Management Information Systems from University of Memphis, in Memphis, TN.

For the past year, she has refined her skill set by way of self-teaching herself Python. Within that time frame, she has branched off to learn Django and game development(PyGames). She has other variety of skills including HTML, WordPress, SharePoint, SQL Server 2000, MS Access, Adobe Photoshop, and Rational.

You will find Tammy hanging out at Women Who Code Python Hack Night as well as DC Python to continue to sharpen her skills in Python.
